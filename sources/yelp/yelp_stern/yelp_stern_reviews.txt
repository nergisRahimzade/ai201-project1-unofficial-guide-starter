Reviewer: Tamara M.

Review: Chipotle-style burrito bowls save an otherwise ho-hum dining hall. This reporter also loves the berry bars. Make sure to get there early because the lines get really long, really quickly.

Reviewer: Kevin L.
Date: 3 years ago
Review: Stern focuses mostly on Latin American and Mexican food. Their taco and burrito bar is the main attraction here. The line moves decently fast but can back up heavily right at noon during the lunch rush. Rice, beans, and meat options are solid, and the guacamole is actually pretty good for a dining hall.

Reviewer: Sarah W.
Date: 4 years ago
Review: Best brunch on the weekends if you like breakfast burritos. During the week it gets pretty repetitive since it's almost always Tex-Mex style food at the main station, but the salad bar has good fresh greens.

Review: Decent food but the seating area can get loud and cramped since it's connected directly to the freshman dorms. The quesadillas are hit or miss depending on who is working the grill line that day.

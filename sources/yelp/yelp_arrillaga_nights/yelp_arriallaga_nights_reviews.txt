Reviewer: Justin W.
Date: 1 year ago
Review: Arrillaga Nights is literally just the normal dining hall but with slightly longer lines and greasy food at 11 PM. It's nice that there is a late-night option on this side of campus since walking all the way to Lakeside or Axe & Palm gets old. The mozzarella sticks and fries are usually warm, but nothing to write home about.

Reviewer: Maya L.
Date: 2 years ago
Review: The burgers here hit the spot after a long study session, but the wait times can get absolutely ridiculous. Sometimes you're standing around for 20 minutes just for a basket of chicken tenders. It's a lifesaver when you're starving late at night, but definitely plan ahead or go during off-peak times.

Reviewer: David K.
